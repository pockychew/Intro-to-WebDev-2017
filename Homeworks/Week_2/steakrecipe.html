Sirloin Steak with Garlic Butter

Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 2 teaspoons of garlic powder
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced

Directions

    1) Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
    2) In a small saucepan, melt butter over medium-low heat with garlic powder and minced garlic.
    3) Sprinkle both sides of each steak with sat and pepper.
    4) Grill steak for 4-5 minuted per side, or to desired doneness. When done, transfer to warmed plate. Brush topsgenerously with garlic butter, and allow to rest for 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
