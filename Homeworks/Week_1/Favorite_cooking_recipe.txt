Sirloin Steak with Garlic Butter

Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup butter
- 2 teaspoons garlic powder
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- Sirloin steak
- Salt and pepper 

Directions:
1) Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
2) In a small saucepan, melt butter over medium-low heat with garlic powder and minced garlic.
3) Sprinkle both sides of each steak with salt and pepper.
4) Grill steak for 4-5 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. When done, transfer to warmed plated. Brush tops
generously with garlic butter, and allow to rest for 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
